Laptop's and Desktop's troubleshooting

If you are experiencing any issues with your laptop or desktop - such as login issues, not connecting to the network, etc. - please follow the troubleshooting steps outlined below.
🗝️ BitLocker Recovery Key

If your device asks you for a BitLocker Recovery Key after a restart, it means that a "safe boot" procedure was interrupted. To resolve this, please locate the key using one of the methods below and then enter it in the recovery screen.

If the problem persists, please contact your IT Engineer directly or submit a ticket through the Helpdesk website for further assistance.
