Laptop's and Desktop's troubleshooting

If you are experiencing any issues with your laptop or desktop - such as login issues, not connecting to the network, etc. - please follow the troubleshooting steps outlined below.
💻 I can't turn on my laptop

Sometimes a laptop screen may remain blank after you press the power button, or you might notice the lights blinking without anything appearing on the display.

First, try connecting your charger to the laptop. The battery may be depleted, even if the device was working properly earlier. Plug it in and wait a few minutes before trying again.

Second, if the laptop still doesn't start, try performing a hard reset. To do this, press and hold the power button for 10 to 15 seconds. Occasionally, a laptop can get stuck in an energy-saving mode that makes it appear off or broken. After holding the button, release it and press it once more to turn the laptop back on. This should help your computer start up normally.

If after trying all the above the laptop still won't turn on, please contact the IT Engineer for further assistance.
